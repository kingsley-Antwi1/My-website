Dr. Zebo Iddiss

General Manager

Dr. Zebo Iddiss brings a wealth of experience in corporate leadership and strategic planning. With a PhD in Business Administration, Zebo has worked across Africa leading major organizational reforms and growth initiatives.

Since joining as General Manager, she has focused on building a strong organizational culture, enhancing customer satisfaction, and improving productivity. She believes in inclusive leadership and empowers every department to thrive.

Outside the office, Dr. Iddiss is a public speaker and a strong advocate for women in leadership. She also enjoys gardening and writing thought leadership pieces on management trends.
