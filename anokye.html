Mr. Anokye Dominic

Secretary

Mr. Anokye Dominic is the backbone of our administrative operations. Known for his organization, efficiency, and calm demeanor under pressure, he ensures the smooth flow of communication across all departments.

Before joining the company, Dominic worked as an executive assistant in both public and private sectors, where he developed a reputation for his professionalism and punctuality. He brings those same values to his current role, where he manages scheduling, correspondence, and internal reporting with precision.

Dominic is also tech-savvy, having introduced digital filing systems that improved document retrieval and security. Outside the office, he enjoys poetry and volunteers as a reading tutor for school children.
