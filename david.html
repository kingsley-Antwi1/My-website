Prof. David Acquah

Managing Director (IT)

Prof. David Acquah is a pioneer in IT transformation and systems design. With over two decades of experience, he has spearheaded several digitalization initiatives across institutions, driving innovation and operational efficiency.

He joined the organization with a vision to integrate smart technology into everyday operations. Under his leadership, the IT department has built a secure, scalable infrastructure that supports remote work and data-driven decision making.

Prof. Acquah is also a visiting professor at various universities, where he advocates for tech inclusion and cybersecurity education. He enjoys drone photography and hiking with his children.
